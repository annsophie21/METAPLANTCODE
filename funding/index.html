Our Funders

MetaPlantCode is funded by Biodiversa+ and co-funded by the European Union. We are grateful for the trust and support that make our work possible.

Biodiversa+ is the European Biodiversity Partnership supporting excellent research and innovation on biodiversity with impact for society and policy.

The European Union co-funds MetaPlantCode through the Horizon Europe programme, supporting innovation and sustainable development across Europe.
