Meet our Principle Contacts

The METAPLANTCODE project is organized into six interdisciplinary Work Packages (WPs), each focusing on a key aspect of our mission: improving plant biodiversity monitoring through DNA metabarcoding. WP1 to 5 cover the scientific and technical core: from field sampling and laboratory workflows to bioinformatic pipelines, data integration, and biodiversity applications. WP6 ensures effective coordination, stakeholder engagement, communication, and outreach. Each Work Package is led by expert teams from across Europe and contributes to our shared goals through defined tasks and deliverables. This collaborative structure allows us to combine diverse expertise, maintain consistent workflows, and deliver meaningful results. The WPs work closely together to ensure that methods, tools, and results are integrated, FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable), and relevant to both science and policy.
